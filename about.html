About Zhioise · 关于青止

Let the beauty of everyday life quietly come into view.

Where Stillness Blooms

A place for incense, adornments, tea—and a quieter state of mind.

Zhioise begins with traditional Chinese incense, accompanied by fragrant adornments and a small selection of Chinese tea. We believe beauty should not belong only to special occasions; it can live in a single wisp of incense, a cup of tea, or an object made by hand, bringing ease and light to ordinary days.

This is not a place of constant updates or endless catalogues. Products are added slowly, guided by season and circumstance. Experiences remain as enduring themes and are arranged by inquiry. Each addition should deepen the whole without disturbing its stillness.

Zhioise does not revolve around one person. The founder is only a small part of the landscape. What matters most are the incense, tea, objects, and the moments created when people meet them. Those who wish to know more may follow the small link below.
