Experiences

Possible gatherings, arranged by inquiry.

No weekly calendar is required. These experience formats remain visible year-round, while dates, group size, venue and details are discussed when someone is ready to gather.

Listening to Incense · 听香

Meet fragrance through attention.

A guided exploration of incense materials, scent development and the quiet act of listening before judging or naming.

Tea Gathering · 茶会

A shared pause around tea.

A small gathering shaped by tea, season, vessels and conversation. Suitable for private groups and intimate cultural events.

Crafting Fragrance · 制香体验

Make a fragrant object by hand.

Possible themes include incense tablets, incense beads and small aromatic objects. The specific project is selected according to time and group.

Seasonal Gatherings · 节气雅集

Tea, incense and making in season.

A flexible format inspired by the twenty-four solar terms. It can combine tea, incense, a small handcraft and seasonal conversation.

Private & Group Formats

Small groups, private sessions and collaborations.

Venue, duration, language and content can be adjusted. No public schedule is needed; each gathering begins with a conversation.

Tea, incense and quiet rituals rooted in Chinese tradition—offered with restraint, attention and room to breathe.
