Behind Zhioise

A practice of tea, incense, and attentive living.

Zhioise is rooted in the belief that traditional culture is not something distant or decorative. It can be lived through the objects we choose, the fragrance that fills a room, and the time we make for tea.

Evelina Zhang

Tea & Incense Practitioner

Evelina has devoted years to the study and practice of Chinese tea culture, traditional incense craftsmanship, and seasonal wellness. Through Zhioise, she brings these quiet traditions into contemporary life with restraint, respect, and care.

Her role remains intentionally behind the work: selecting, making, teaching, and creating spaces where others may experience stillness in their own way.
